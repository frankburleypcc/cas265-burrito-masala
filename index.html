Burrito Masala

Welcome to Burrito Masala!

We are a fusion food cart located in downtown Portland, OR that specializes in delicious southern Indian (Kerala) and Mexican foods... stuffed in a flour tortilla! Diners can also opt to go gluten free with our bowl options. We’re run by a husband and wife team (James Beard award-winning chefs Prasad Varma and Ramona Varma-Hernandez). Enjoy!
